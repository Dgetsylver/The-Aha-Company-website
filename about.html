Our Story

Building the Future of Blockchain Infrastructure

Founded in 2021, The Aha Company is a team of seasoned blockchain developers shipping institutional-grade infrastructure.

We don't just write code. We architect systems that institutions rely on.
From Stellar to Ethereum, we build multi-chain infrastructure that scales.
Security, compliance, performance. Every line of code is built to last.

Our Journey

2021

Founded with a mission to build developer-first blockchain tooling and infrastructure for the Stellar ecosystem.

2022

Shipped the Stellar CLI and core developer tooling, establishing ourselves as key contributors to the Stellar ecosystem.

2023

Partnered with SocGen on the EURCV stablecoin initiative, bridging institutional finance with blockchain technology.

2024

Launched Architect Programs, helping enterprises design and deploy custom blockchain solutions at scale.

2025

Surpassed 40+ companies supported, delivering mission-critical infrastructure across DeFi, tokenization, and payments.

2026

Expanding multi-chain capabilities across Stellar, Ethereum, NEAR, Cosmos, and emerging ecosystems.
